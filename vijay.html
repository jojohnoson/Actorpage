Vijay

Vijay is an Indian actor and playback singer who works predominantly in Tamil cinema. He is one of the highest-paid actors in South India and has a significant fan following. Vijay made his acting debut as a child artist in the film "Vetri" (1984) and later appeared in several films before establishing himself as a leading actor with the film "Poove Unakkaga" (1996). Over the years, he has delivered numerous blockbuster hits such as "Ghilli," "Thuppakki," "Mersal," and "Master." Known for his charismatic screen presence, dance skills, and philanthropic activities, Vijay continues to be a prominent figure in the Indian film industry.
